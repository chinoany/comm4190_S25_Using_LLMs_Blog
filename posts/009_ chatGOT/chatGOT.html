ChatGOT: ChatGPT Writes an imagined Season 9 Game of Thrones Novella!

Introduction

Have you ever wished an epic series never ended, craving just one more season after the screen fades to black? As a fan of the infamous Game of Thrones series I found myself in this position after the show ended abrutly and rushed in the eighth and final season. With a single prompt I asked ChatGPT to produce a mini book version of an imagined Season 9 of Game of Thrones and package it as a PDF. After downloading and reading the 8 chapter novella, I comment on whether it scratches that post-finale itch!

Experiment Setup

  • Model: GPT-4o
  • Prompt: “Write a short novella (~1,700 words) that tells a complete Season 9 story of Game of Thrones, broken into chapters.” Format output for PDF, no pictures.

You get to be one of the first to read the Game of Thrones Season 9 mini book!

from IPython.display import IFrame, display
import base64, pathlib

pdf_path = pathlib.Path("GoT_Season9_Novella.pdf")

data = base64.b64encode(pdf_path.read_bytes()).decode()

display(IFrame(f"data:application/pdf;base64,{data}", width=900, height=550))

Observations After Reading

  1. Lore continuity is spot on. The novella picks up right after Bran’s coronation, weaves in politics (Northern autonomy, Iron Bank debts) and magic (frost spiders) all true to how Season 8 left off.
  2. Character voices feel authentic.
  3. Not as detailed as I would like. At ~1,700 words somehow this novella reads more like a draft than a proper Season 9 mini book.
  4. New myth arc. The “Frost Shepherd” gives the story a new existential threat different from the defeated Night King which I guess makes the plot line more interesting.

Key Takeaways!

  1. PDF generation is quite easy. One prompt + one code cell = downloadable mini-book in minutes.
  2. Depth vs. breadth trade off. Short-form prose can’t replicate eight hours of screen time, expect basic summary not heavy plot line.
  3. Great start! The draft functions as a storyboard: we can expand on the scenes and add more dialogue!

Why Does This Matter?

Fan communities, screenwriting classes, and others can leverage LLMs to keep beloved fictional worlds alive by generating novellas, radio drama scripts, or comic outlines overnight.
